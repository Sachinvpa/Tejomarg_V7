Our Focus Areas

Four areas. One mission.

Education, Health, Women Empowerment, and Clean Water — chosen because progress in any one is held back by gaps in the others. We work where they intersect.

Education

Strengthening the institutions that shape the next generation — from engineering colleges to government schools — through faculty training, applied learning programmes, and student opportunity.

See education programmes

Health

Working with primary health centres and community workers to improve preventive care, maternal & child health, and access to basic medical services in underserved areas.

See health programmes

Women Empowerment

Supporting self-help groups, skill-development cohorts, and entrepreneurship pathways that translate confidence into income — and income into long-term independence.

See women's programmes

Clean Water

Restoring access to safe drinking water in rural Karnataka through community-led infrastructure, school-based filtration, and water-quality monitoring in partnership with local administration.

See water programmes
◆ World Environment Day · 2026

Government school students explore the Vaccine Depot urban forest, Belagavi.

To mark World Environment Day, we led 62 students from Government High School, Rani Channamma Nagar, on a guided walk through Belagavi's Vaccine Depot urban forest — a hands-on session on native trees, the part green cover plays in clean air, water, and soil, and why protecting it matters for their own future. Curiosity in the field, not just lessons on a board.
